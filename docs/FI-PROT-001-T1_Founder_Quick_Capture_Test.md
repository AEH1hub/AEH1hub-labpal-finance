# FI-PROT-001-T1 --- Founder Quick Capture Test

**Status:** ACTIVE\
**Test type:** Real founder behavioral probe\
**Prototype:** FI-PROT-001\
**Rule:** Use a genuine current thought. Do not reconstruct an old trade
and do not invent a synthetic thesis.

## Pre-registered failure rule

T1 fails if **any** of the following occurs:

1.  Quick Capture takes more than 60 seconds.
2.  The capture is abandoned before preservation.
3.  The founder feels the urge to use Notes, WhatsApp, or another faster
    capture tool because LabPal is slowing the thought down.

The failure rule is fixed before the test result is known.

## Test procedure

1.  Extract the FI-PROT-001 prototype folder.
2.  Open `index.html` in a browser.
3.  Wait until a genuine market/investment thought occurs.
4.  Do not pre-write the thought elsewhere.
5.  Enter the instrument or subject.
6.  Enter the raw thought exactly as it occurs.
7.  Add an evidence fragment only if naturally available.
8.  Click `Preserve capture`.
9.  Record the capture time shown.
10. Stop. Do not clean the wording before reporting T1.

## Decision rule

-   **PASS:** 60 seconds or less, not abandoned, and no urge to switch
    to a faster capture tool.
-   **FAIL:** any pre-registered failure condition occurs.
-   **INVALID TEST:** reconstructed historical trade, synthetic thesis,
    or pre-written thought.

# FI-FOUND-001 — Livigence Architecture

**Status:** CANDIDATE — FOUNDATION REVIEW REQUIRED
**Scope:** Domain-independent LabPal architecture

## Purpose

The Livigence Architecture defines how LabPal preserves reality, connects evidence, explains understanding and evolves responsibly.

Finance is the first proving domain. It is not the complete identity of LabPal.

## Architectural order

1. Foundation
2. Governance
3. Understanding
4. Evidence
5. Operations
6. Domains
7. Product
8. Company

No lower layer may silently redefine a higher layer.

## Core responsibility

LabPal must be able to explain:

- why the current understanding exists;
- which evidence supports it;
- which evidence challenges it;
- what remains unknown;
- what changed from the previous understanding;
- what evidence could change the current understanding again.

## Livigence Graph

The Livigence Graph connects:

- needs;
- questions;
- observations;
- evidence;
- claims;
- sources;
- unknowns;
- contradictions;
- interpretations;
- decisions;
- reviews;
- revisions;
- history.

Connections do not automatically establish truth.

Selection does not equal verification.

Agreement does not equal validity.

Profit does not equal proof.

## Institutional memory

LabPal distinguishes:

### Memory

Preserves what occurred.

### Knowledge

Preserves facts, sources and established relationships.

### Understanding

Connects evidence, context, contradictions and unknowns into an explainable working interpretation.

### Wisdom

Supports appropriate action within explicit authority and evidence boundaries.

Wisdom must not be simulated through unsupported certainty.

## Evolution rule

Reality may change the architecture.

The architecture must never change reality.

New responsibilities must be earned through demonstrated evidence.

## Autonomy boundary

LabPal may autonomously:

- schedule observations;
- classify operational states;
- preserve evidence;
- detect staleness;
- surface contradictions;
- identify missing evidence;
- produce a working interpretation;
- propose corrections;
- perform low-risk deterministic corrections when authorized.

LabPal may not autonomously:

- declare its interpretation verified;
- rewrite primary evidence;
- change constitutional authority;
- issue financial recommendations;
- forecast price direction as fact;
- hide failures;
- silently revise institutional memory;
- deploy high-impact changes without review.

## Domain inheritance

Every domain must inherit the Foundation, Governance, Understanding and Evidence layers.

A domain may add specialized rules, but it may not weaken foundational boundaries.

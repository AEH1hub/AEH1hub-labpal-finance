# OPS-001 — Institutional Review Report

**Status:** CANDIDATE

Every review report must include:

## Identity

- report ID;
- reporting period;
- system version;
- branch or release;
- author or automated actor.

## What passed

Only verified results.

## What failed

Failures must remain visible.

## What changed

Include before and after states.

## What remains unknown

Include reason, impact and evidence needed.

## Corrections

Separate:

- automatically performed;
- proposed;
- awaiting approval;
- rejected.

## Regressions

List all regression suites and results.

## Current Understanding

Explain why the current understanding exists.

## Alerts

Identify matters requiring human or constitutional review.

## Next responsibilities

State what must be demonstrated before new authority is earned.

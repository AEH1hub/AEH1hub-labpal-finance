# OPS-PLAY-007 — Merge Review Playbook

**Status:** CANDIDATE — OPERATIONAL REVIEW REQUIRED

## Purpose

Determine whether a branch may be merged without violating scope, evidence, history, or constitutional boundaries.

## Procedure

1. Fetch remote state.
2. Confirm base and head.
3. Compare commits and changed filenames.
4. Review diff statistics and whitespace.
5. Run relevant regressions.
6. Confirm branch-only claims are not described as merged.
7. Check secret exposure.
8. Confirm failures and contradictions remain preserved.
9. Confirm candidate documents are not described as active engines.
10. Record approval, rejection, or revision required with reasons.

## Approval criteria

The diff matches purpose, tests pass or exceptions are reviewed, no unrelated files or secrets exist, authority remains intact, documentation matches reality, and the branch is mergeable.

## Rejection criteria

Scope drift, unexplained failing tests, hidden history, silently altered evidence, incorrect target branch, or unresolved material conflict.

# FI-FUNDING-001 — Pre-Seed Funding and Valuation Context

**Status:** PRESERVED CANDIDATE CONTEXT — NOT VERIFIED  
**Decision status:** NOT ADOPTED  
**Purpose:** Preserve a strategic discussion for later research.

## Current company-stage interpretation

LabPal is currently:

- pre-seed;
- pre-revenue;
- led by a solo founder;
- supported by a working technical prototype;
- supported by one merged live-provider integration;
- supported by founder-only behavioral evidence;
- not yet supported by external-user evidence.

## Potential strengths discussed

- verified live evidence integration;
- adversarial and regression validation;
- explicit provenance and uncertainty handling;
- differentiated principles such as `Profit ≠ Proof` and `Fact ≠ Interpretation`;
- disciplined separation between reported and verified progress.

## Material risks discussed

- zero external users;
- solo-founder concentration;
- unresolved Swiss regulatory classification;
- one provider and one endpoint only;
- no longitudinal outcome evidence;
- no demonstrated willingness to pay.

## Unverified external-market claims

A prior discussion suggested that some pre-seed software companies may raise approximately:

- `$500K–$3M`;
- on approximately `$3M–$10M` valuation caps.

These figures have not been independently sourced or validated for LabPal, Switzerland, fintech, or the current fundraising market.

They must not be presented as:

- LabPal's valuation;
- a fundraising entitlement;
- an investor offer;
- verified market data.

## Value-creating evidence priorities

Before serious valuation work:

1. obtain external-user evidence;
2. resolve the Swiss regulatory perimeter with qualified counsel;
3. complete longitudinal live-provider observation;
4. establish a clear target user and painful problem;
5. gather evidence of repeated use or willingness to pay;
6. review current comparable funding data from credible sources.

## Preserved conclusion

The discussion is strategically useful, but no precise LabPal valuation has been earned.

This document preserves questions and hypotheses for later investigation. It does not establish financial advice, fundraising terms, or company valuation.
